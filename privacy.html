Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Fabric Technology Ltd. ("Fabric", "we", "us", or "our"), a company incorporated under the laws of the British Virgin Islands, is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use the Fabric platform, APIs, dashboard, and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (if provided for account creation or billing)
  • Ethereum wallet address (if you authenticate via SIWE)
  • Organisation name (if applicable)
  • Billing information processed through Stripe (we do not store full payment card details)

1.2 Usage Data

When you interact with the Service, we automatically collect:

  • API request logs (timestamps, endpoints accessed, response codes, latency)
  • Request routing data (provider selections, trust score evaluations)
  • Rate limit and quota usage
  • IP addresses and user agent strings
  • Dashboard session data (pages viewed, features used)

1.3 On-Chain Data

Certain interactions with the Service involve public blockchain transactions. On-chain data includes:

  • Wallet addresses associated with transactions
  • Transaction hashes and amounts
  • Smart contract interactions (provider registration, reputation updates, identity records)

On-chain data is inherently public, immutable, and not within Fabric's control to modify or delete. This Privacy Policy governs only the off-chain data we collect and process.

1.4 Provider Data

If you register as a service provider, we additionally collect:

  • Provider endpoint URLs and configuration
  • Service descriptions and category information
  • Performance metrics (uptime, latency, success rates)
  • Feedback and trust score data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To process transactions and manage billing
  • To calculate and update trust scores for providers
  • To route requests to appropriate providers based on your preferences
  • To monitor for abuse, fraud, and violations of our Acceptable Use Policy
  • To generate aggregated, anonymised analytics about platform usage
  • To communicate with you about your account, service updates, and security notices
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other policies

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the Service as agreed in our Terms of Service
  • Legitimate interests: Processing necessary for platform security, fraud prevention, service improvement, and aggregated analytics
  • Legal obligation: Processing required to comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Consent: Where required for specific processing activities, which you may withdraw at any time

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating the Service (e.g., Stripe for payment processing, cloud infrastructure providers, monitoring services). These providers are bound by contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • Providers on the network: When you route a request through Fabric, the selected provider will receive the data necessary to fulfil that request. You should review each provider's own privacy policy.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
  • Aggregated data: We may share anonymised, aggregated statistics that cannot identify any individual.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specific retention periods:

  • Account information: retained until account deletion, plus 90 days
  • API request logs: 90 days in detailed form, then aggregated
  • Billing records: 7 years (as required for tax and financial compliance)
  • Security and abuse logs: 1 year
  • On-chain data: permanent and immutable (not within our control)

After account deletion, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law or necessary for legitimate business purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • API key hashing and secure credential storage
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring
  • Infrastructure hosted on reputable cloud providers with industry-standard security certifications

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International Data Transfers

Fabric is incorporated in the British Virgin Islands and operates infrastructure globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including the United States and the European Union. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@fabriclayer.dev. We will respond within 30 days. Note that certain rights cannot be exercised with respect to on-chain data, which is publicly accessible and immutable by design.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The Fabric dashboard may use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. If we implement analytics, we will use privacy-respecting solutions and update this policy accordingly.

10. Children

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through the Service dashboard at least 30 days before they take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Fabric Technology Ltd.
Email: privacy@fabriclayer.dev
